There are many financial calculators in the world but ours is slightly different. If you are planning on buying multiple products over the next couple of years, our calculator can help you understand how much you would need to save and how long it would take for you to reach your financial goals. Have you ever faced any situation where you are confused on how much to save for your future goals or how long it will take for you to purchase a certain item? Introduction
In order to help those in poverty and the economically unstable, we have created a project to help them become more financially aware. Our website helps users keep track of their money and brings them to success with financial advice and savings tips from blogs. What it is
Our mission is to help people spend money wisely and be financially literate. We would like to create individual savings plans for each user to help them achieve their financial goal, provide informative blogs with financial advice, and offer exclusive savings plans from our partner banks.
